Creating an Exploration

After you've played an exploration, why not try your hand at making one? To do so, click the "Create" button in the top navbar.

This will open the Exploration Editor page.

The page asks for a two things:

  • A title. Pick a short, descriptive title that conveys the essence of your exploration.
  • An introduction. What is the exploration supposed to help the learner learn?

Once you've done that, a blue button will appear in the bottom left. Click it to add interactions such as equations, math expressions, maps, buttons and menus. Congratulations, you've created a new exploration!
