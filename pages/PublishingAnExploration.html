Publishing an Exploration

At present, explorations are not world-viewable when created. In order to make an exploration visible to other people, you will need to publish it. You can do this by clicking the 'Publish' button in the exploration editor navbar.

Note: At present, once an exploration is published, it cannot be unpublished. However, it can still be incrementally improved.

Note that you can only publish an exploration that is structurally valid; this is done to avoid learners getting stuck and frustrated while playing it. The exploration warnings in the top right of the exploration editor will display any issues detected for your exploration, such as isolated cards and feedback-less self-loops. After you fix these, the exploration will become publishable.
